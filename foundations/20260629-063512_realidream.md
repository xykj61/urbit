## The Name

Realidream carries two roots, and the work lives in holding them together. The first is reality: ground that holds, in bounded memory, deterministic execution, and one faithful history that anyone can replay. The second is dream: a free and open surface, updates that stream as you work, and room to create within those clear bounds. Reality keeps the dream honest; the dream keeps the reality worth building. One tool, both at once.

## One Surface, Two Faces

Today the editor and the browser are two separate worlds. One is where a person writes a program; the other is where they read the web. Realidream makes them a single surface. You read a page and open its source in the same place. The program you write and the preview you watch are two views of one living thing, so a change in the code reaches the page with nothing fragile stretched between them. The reading and the writing become one motion.

## Over One Graph

Beneath that surface lies a single directed graph — [the graph beneath the surface](20260629-063512_the-graph-beneath-the-surface.md) in full. Every piece is a node: a line of a program, a fragment of a page, a control on the screen. Every dependency is an edge. When something changes, the change flows along the edges, and only the nodes downstream of it recompute. Two sources can feed one node, so separate inputs fuse cleanly into a single view. The frameworks of today redraw whole branches of a tree on every small change, carrying hidden state and unpredictable timing; Realidream flows each update along the edges that truly depend on it. Less is redrawn, so the surface stays quick, and the same events always reach the same view, so it stays trustworthy.

## The Backend, in Our Own Modules

The graph rests on three Rye OS modules, each doing one thing well. Tally bounds the memory: a known ceiling of nodes, named up front, so the surface grows only within a budget and never exhausts the machine by surprise. Mantra keeps the history: an append-only log of signed facts, where every node's lineage is a chain anyone can replay, and nothing is quietly rewritten. Tablecloth is the fold over that log: the deterministic table that turns the facts into a view, so the same history always yields the same surface. Bounds, history, and a faithful fold — together they are the graph beneath Realidream.

## Skate Shares the Graph

Skate, the visual surface, draws from the very same graph. A sketch, a program, and a page become three views of one model, and a change made in one can flow to the others. The person moves among them without ever leaving the single source of truth, because there is only one.

## Media Through Maitreya

When Realidream plays audio or video — Linengrow rooms, civic streams, or embedded media in a page — playback rides **Maitreya**, the Rye OS media driver. Maitreya's possession is **time**: one clock for decode, sync, and bounded buffers, while Brushstroke owns space. Lotus authors the studio hot loop above Maitreya; Realidream owns the fold-to-frame path in the living surface. Codecs stay royalty-free host seams; one media path keeps buffers bounded under Tally rather than scattered across ad hoc libraries.

## Reality, Kept by Construction

The discipline that holds the kernel holds the surface too. The same inputs always produce the same view. The node count carries a fixed ceiling, and the graph's indexing wraps cleanly, so the edges of the structure hold no surprises. Speed arrives because little needs redrawing, rather than through cleverness laid over a fragile core. Correctness lives in the shape of the thing, and the interface inherits it.

## Where It Sits Among the Rest

Aurora is the dawn the machine wakes into, the boot that brings the system to life; Realidream is the surface a person meets once it is awake. [Rishi](20260628-133212_a-home-in-plain-text.md) is the calm shell beside it, a field of text and a few sharp commands. Comlink is the single wire the whole system speaks through, and only when you choose. Realidream is an open browser as much as an editor — a bounded, honest surface over its own graph and the open web it chooses to render, rather than a from-scratch engine rivaling the few the world maintains: it reads the open web, holds a person's own data in their own hands, carries no advertising anyone asked to be shown, and settles in the local currency through the same shared ledger [anywhere we are found](20260628-124512_anywhere-we-are-found.md) already names. The intelligence that helps you write and read flows in from [Lantern, Lattice, and Anvil](20260629-063512_lantern-lattice-anvil.md), a guest on the surface rather than a watcher behind it.
