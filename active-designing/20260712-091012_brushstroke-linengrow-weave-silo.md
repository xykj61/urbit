## Six invariants (design law — comments beside the constants)

1. **Gentle lightness spread** — about 66 points (page ≈ L83 light, ≈ L17 dark) so texture stays readable rather than glared out
2. **One warm hue family across both modes** — the light goes down; the cloth stays the same
3. **Elevation as finer weave** — never a drop shadow
4. **Thread spacing** — felt as a 3–6px range; **4px** chosen (`thread_px`)
5. **Tablecloth-and-writing-paper hierarchy** — cloth holds; paper receives the writing
6. **Low blue throughout** — warm family survives Mantrapod’s reflective glass; add a **4-gray reflective fallback tier**

Hex values are tonight’s weather; these six lines are the law. Stroke-reveal motion carries a **reduced-motion** token from birth.
