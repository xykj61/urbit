## Claim

When the SETU6 lab card is **stand-in**, Desk **refuses** framed TX. When the card is **armed** (`usb0` / `ncm*`), Desk prepares the SETU5 framed payload and attempts an iface-bound UDP datagram on port **38503**.

## Thin face (approved)

| Path | Behavior |
|------|----------|
| Stand-in / no iface | Gate refuses · host SKIP GREEN |
| Armed + iface | Prepare frame · `SO_BINDTODEVICE` · UDP send |
| Bind/send incomplete | Honest GREEN naming `send-failed` (CAP/gadget) |

Live “sent” on a real Pixel CDC-NCM gadget or QEMU stays the next eyes lap once an iface exists.
