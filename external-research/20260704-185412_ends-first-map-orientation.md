**First: age is a proxy, and the doors are the law.** The real promotion doors are already seated — a Kaeden word, a measured red, a second consumer — and a middle document walks through any of them regardless of its birthday. The newest mines the middle freely and cites it; it simply owes it no stops. Deprioritized as work, never as ground.

## The Long Arc, in Kaeden's Own Shape

Over time the two ends converge on the middle. The oldest end supplies proven ground — the foundations, the disciplines, the seeds. The newest end supplies momentum — the current laps, each small and green. They meet at the middle's larger systems exactly when grown-from-simples permits a larger system to exist, which is Gall's Law wearing a calendar. Deprioritized, read rightly, means **reached, never raided**: the held middle is the destination the ends are growing toward, and it will be arrived at whole rather than seized early.
