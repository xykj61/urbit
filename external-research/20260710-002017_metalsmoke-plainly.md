## In One Breath

**Metal** is the real machine — not a stub, not a replay. **Smoke** is the craftsman's smallest power-on check: is the whole alive at all?

**Metalsmoke** is the smallest proof that our software and the world still agree — run on true metal, at the one seam where **only the world** can answer.

Everything that *can* be proven headless belongs in the **happy zone** (fast parity witnesses). Metalsmoke keeps only the **thin edge**: a few honest integrated checks, deliberately few.

---

## The Everyday Picture

Think of a workshop whose tools are proven on the bench indoors — every gear measured, every fold rerun — and a tiny outdoor check at the door: open the real window, draw one real frame, close cleanly. That outdoor check is metalsmoke. If it fails, you know the fault is at the world-seam, because the indoor bench already went green.

The drawn terminal's first metalsmoke opens a genuine compositor connection, draws a real frame, and asks: does the surface appear, does the frame land, does exit leave the seat clean? The wrapper `tools/slc2a_ring3_metal.rish` reads those answers. Headless session witnesses still prove the conversation without a window — that is the happy zone doing its job.

---

## Three Marks to Earn the Name

A metalsmoke check needs **all three**:

| Mark | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **World alone can answer** | Compositor, interrupt, host descriptor — anything a stub could fake stays inward |
| **Smallest slice** | One connection, one frame, one clean release — never a feature tour |
| **Witnessed** | A wrapper asserts the answers the same way on every bench |

Refuse the name the moment a **mirrored collaboration/contract pair** could prove the same thing — that pair belongs on the seam, not on the thin edge.

---

## Rhythm with Parity

| Layer | Job |
|-------|-----|
| **Parity (happy zone)** | Fast, isolated witnesses on every push — folds, values, headless sessions |
| **Metalsmoke (thin edge)** | When a world-seam opens, closes, or is touched — few checks, world-truth only |
| **Human hand** | Live confirmation on the same seam when a door closes (Lap 3 pattern) |

SLC-2a Lap 3 already breathed this pattern: headless green in parity, metalsmoke green on the GNOME Wayland host, live five-step as the hand.

## What the Audit Rule Already Seated

The **standing audit rule** (ratified `20260703.191312`): every new seam ships its mirrored collaboration/contract pair; integrated checks live only on the thin edge; one pair per season (RW-1 and RW-2 already landed).

What still waits for a separate Kaeden word is folding that pairing habit into **TAME guidance** prose — the horizon row on TASKS, not a reopen of metalsmoke's definition.
