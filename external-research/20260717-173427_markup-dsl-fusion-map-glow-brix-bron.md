## The Honest Question

How should Glow fuse **Glow** (language), **Brix** (composition), **Bron** (data notation), **Nix/infuse** ideas, **Clay/Ford** roles, **family runes**, **Udon/Sail** (and other Hoon/Zuse/Arvo markup DSLs), and day-to-day **files/** paths — without becoming a second Urbit or a paste of Zuse?

The answer is a **pipeline**, not a rename pile. Each teacher keeps its gratitude license; each Glow piece keeps its name.

## The Pipeline (one spine)

```
prose / mark source
    → Scribble (markdown-shaped mark; Ford "mark" role)
    → Brix (compose + declare; Ford/Nix-infuse role)
    → Bron-shaped values (data at the seam; JSON/ZON-like job)
    → Mantra (store + marks + revisions; Clay role)
    → Sala / Skate / Brushstroke (surface)
         ↘ Glow runes lower to Rye under TAME (language path)
```

An Acme employee reading this can hold one sentence: **write in marks, compose in Brix, carry Bron, remember in Mantra, show in Sala, program in Glow.**
