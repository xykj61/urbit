## What the Teachers Teach, Siloed

The agent-model lineage: an app is a long-lived agent whose whole state is a fold over typed
events; a poke is a signed fact arriving, a peek is a projection read, and an upgrade is just
another event the fold understands — so apps inherit history, sync, and crash-recovery from
the substrate instead of building four private copies. The immediate-mode lineage: a frame is
a pure function of state — no retained scene graph to drift, every redraw honest — which is
already Skate's whole personality. Neither teacher is named in the body; both are thanked in
their room.

## Puddle, Grown from Its Own Charter

Puddle today is the bounded place where unknown things run without reaching the house. That
*is* the app store's hard part, already named — concentric growth from harbor to marketplace.
The growth is concentric. An **app bundle** is a Brix-described, content-addressed set of resins —
code, assets, manifest — signed by its maker's Kumara key. **Install** is a season: dock the
bundle, verify signatures and budgets cold, seat it in a puddle with named ceilings (memory
garden, wire budget, capability list — the chosen fold bounding bandwidth by construction).
**Run** is the agent model on our spine: the app's state folds from its own event log; the
window is Skate drawing that fold, frame as pure function. **Upgrade** is a new bundle fact;
the old one keeps working until the maker's signed migration says otherwise —
accrete-never-break, enforced at the harbor door. **Purchase** is a MALA fact when the
maker asks a price, with the receipt provable the SLC-L1 way. And **moderation** is chosen
labels on the render, never deletion of the log — the memo's geometry, kept.

## The first lap, Honestly Small

One fixture bundle; one witness proving the harbor refuses a tampered resin, refuses an
unbudgeted manifest, seats a good bundle in a puddle, and runs it to one drawn frame. No
network, no payment, no upgrade — those are later laps, each behind its own gate. Simple,
lovable (an app you made, running in a puddle you own), complete at its scope.
