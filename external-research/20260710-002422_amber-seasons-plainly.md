## In One Breath

A **season** is a bounded interval with a **named opening**, a **stated budget**, and a **clean close** that returns everything it held.

Gardens bound **space** that way. The bench bounds **attention** that way. Amber bounds **custody** that way — Tally's discipline applied to time.

Amber is **cellar software**. Between seasons it **sleeps**: bare drives shelved, catalog readable warm without a dock, keys cold. Activity is never a daemon — only a season opened by a hand.

---

## The Everyday Picture

Imagine a cold cellar of sealed jars (resins). Most of the year you leave the door shut — preservation is the discipline of *not touching*. When you need to seal a new batch, scrub what already rests, or restore a jar, you **open a season**: walk to the dock with your own hands, speak the one job for this visit, work within the budget you named, power down, sleeve the drive, close.

Nothing opens the cellar for you — no calendar, no plug-in whisper, no helpful background process. The shell may *notice* a docked drive and quietly offer; it acts only when asked.

---

## Three Kinds of Amber Season

| Season | Heat | Leaves on the log |
|--------|------|-------------------|
| **Seal** | Signing key warm only for the seconds of the signature | Archive record + write-time verification |
| **Scrub** | Cold for the walk; one brief unlock to sign the reviewed batch | Fresh verification — or a loud failed one with a correction |
| **Restore** | Drive passphrase warms to read; signing key stays cold | Recovering hand's answer, bit-faithful against the seal |

Warm for seconds, never for the session. A season may also **decline** — a signed refusal with a receipt, the same shape as resin-refusal.

## What Lap One Already Bought

Amber's first lap (parity **144**) was one complete seal-verify-restore turn at fixture scale: resins under `resins/`, manifest digest pinned to an outside oracle (`openssl`), restore bit-faithful, tamper refused. That was the **working seed** — the smallest season that could honestly close.

Later seasons wait in the functional spec: live bare-drive seal, vault at rest, Kumara-native signing, quarterly scrub of real archives. Each arrives as its own lap inside its own season — nothing tonight promises them.

Amphora (vessel software) carries resins **in motion** when a second dock needs them; that pour is a later lap, not the cellar waking itself.
