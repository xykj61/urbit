# River — Wayland compositor (Isaac Freund)

**License:** GNU General Public License v3.0 — **not linked into Rye OS**  
**Role for us:** Lodestar for the eventual **Rye-native compositor** Aurora will need on bare metal. Held at clean-room distance because of copyleft.

**Clean room:** We study River's public design and documentation; we never copy its source into our modules.

---

## License stance

River is **GPLv3**. We honor it as inspiration for compositor shape — tiling, riverctl, tags — and we build our own compositor vocabulary in the clean room when the kernel earns a display path.

The GPL requires that derivative works of GPL code be GPL. We do not link River; we learn from how a compositor written in our systems language is shaped.

For the full license text, see the [GNU GPL v3](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0.html) and River's upstream repository.
