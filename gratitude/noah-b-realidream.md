**Sources:**

- Bandcamp: <https://thisisnoahb.bandcamp.com/>

Our **Realidream** horizon lane keeps that pairing: ground that holds, surface that streams, one tool both at once ([`../foundations/20260629-063512_realidream.md`](../foundations/20260629-063512_realidream.md)).
