# REAPER — Cockos digital audio workstation

**License:** Proprietary commercial software (Cockos). **We do not link REAPER into Rye OS.** We study its **public web documentation** (local copies in `reaper/docs/`) and honor the workflow discipline it represents.

**Clean room:** Study and run REAPER as a tool on the host; never copy its source or UI assets into **Lotus** or any Rye module.

---

## Role for us

REAPER is the lodestar for **Lotus** — our own DAW concept — on workflow and craft:

- One small, fast binary; everything editable while transport runs
- Deep action/script extensibility (ReaScript: EEL, Lua, Python)
- JSFX — user-programmable effects without stopping audio
- Honest multitrack editing: items, takes, lanes, routing, nested folders
- Portable, forum-supported, community-written user guide

Lotus learns REAPER's *discipline* (hot loop, extensibility, item model) in the clean room; **Brushstroke** draws its surfaces; **Genesis** holds the why Zig exists.
