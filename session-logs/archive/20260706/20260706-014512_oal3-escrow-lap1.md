## The Invariants, Stated Before the Algorithm

Escrow touches the money fold, so its invariants are named first and the fold is built to preserve them. The escrow balance opens at zero, rises by the held amount, and closes at zero on release, so the sum of holds equals the sum of releases across a completed bounty and no unit is created or destroyed. The escrow balance is never negative at any step. A release reaches only the accepted applicant, only for the accepted request, only in the held amount, and only after a matching hold. A hold requires a prior acceptance, and neither a hold nor a release repeats. Every escrow fact is poster-signed and every amount stays within the receipt-core bound. The whole story is internal — own log, own keys, test units, no external chain.
